About Me

My name is Victoire Coronas and I am French student at University College Utrecht in the Netherlands (2028). My major is in cognitive neuroscience and physiology and I am interested in cartography. My work on this website was done in the context of a Geographic Informations Systems course at UCU and focuses on different mapping projects to practice my cartographic design work.

Map Portfolio

Chloropleth map in QGIS

This chloropleth map illustrates the proportion of seats held by women in national parliaments across countries using data from the UN from 2025.

Vector data and analysis

On this page you will find a spatial analysis of the walking accesibility from residencies to schoold in Utrecht, Netherlands using isochrone layers in QGIS. You will also find a density map using volunteered geographic information (VGI) exploring perceived plesantness by gender in Utrecht.

Raster Elevation Models

This map shows the elevation of the Kleine Dommel river terrain in the Netherlands. On this project's page you can also find a map showing the elevation including the surface levels (such as trees and buildings), as well as a map comparing this terrain to an old map of the same area in 1897.

Vegetation Indices

This project maps the true color composites of the Greater Rio de Janeiro region in 2018 and in 2025 using the data from the Sentinel-2 satellite. Using this data, we also compared the vegetation indexes of the same area in 2018 and 2025 allowing us to see to what extent deforrestation or urbanization affected this area in the past 7 years.

Land Cover Classification

On this page you will find maps of Utrecht, Netherlands displaying land cover classifications in several different ways.

Multi-criteria Analysis

Here, you will find an explenation of what a multi-criteria analysis is as well as an example of the kind of project one can make using this technique.
